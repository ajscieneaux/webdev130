Hello there! My name is Amy. I used to fly hot air balloons. In the picture to the right, I am the person on the left. On right is my younger sister.
I took my sister for a ride in my hot air balloon when I lived in Utah. She is afraid of heights so it was quite a challenge for her to come with me. We had a great time though.

I am building this page to share information about hot air balloing and some of the things I like about ballooning.


A few things you may not know:

  • To have the least amount of unexpected air disturbance balloons fly in early morning or late evening.
  • Tethered rides on a hot air balloon cause more stress on the balloon than free flight.
  • Flying a hot air balloon is a lot of work, but is also a lot of fun.

Utah/Texas/Idaho

Texas is great - even with it's hot summer months. I miss the Utah and Idaho snow at Christmas, and I miss the cool summer nights, but I love being with my family and feel like Texas is home. Texas is the largest state in the lower 48 states. It is bordered by Louisiana, Arkansas, Oklahoma, and New Mexico. It also shares a border with Mexico. It has a coastline on the Gulf of Mexico as well. The state capital is Austin. Several other states could fit within the state of Texas and still have room left over.
