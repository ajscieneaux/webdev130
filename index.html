I was born in Utah, grew up in Idaho, and now live in Texas.

Texas is great - even with it's hot summer months. I miss the Utah and Idaho snow at Christmas, and I miss the cool summer nights, but I love being with my family and feel like Texas is home. Texas is the largest state in the lower 48 states. It is bordered by Louisiana, Arkansas, Oklahoma, and New Mexico. It also shares a border with Mexico. It has a coastline on the Gulf of Mexico as well. The state capital is Austin. Several other states could fit within the state of Texas and still have room left over.
