TRIPS

We have the adventure trip for you! Here are our most popular adventures.
IF you would prefer another adventure, please contact us and we can custom make an adventure for you!

Half Day Rapids Adventure:

Our half day wild rapid adventures is 4-6 hour trip that includes a variety of rapid from class I to class III (easy to situations where you will likely get wet). All equipment needed on the river is provided as well as transporation from landing area back to main starting dock where there is plenty of room to park your vehicles.

Full Day Rapids Adventure:

Our full day wild rapid adventures is an 8 hour trip that includes a variety of rapid from class I to class IV (rapids start of easy but continue to some difficult rapids and intense white water). Included is this trip are the following: all equipment needed on the river, lunch and transporation from landing area back to main starting dock. Please bring sunscreen and water to drink. Do not bring anything you don't want lost in the river.

Overnight Adventure:

Our overnight wild rapid adventures is a two day trip that includes a variety of rapid from class I to class iV (rapids start of easy but continue to some difficult rapids and intense white water). Included in this trip are the following: All equipment needed on the river, dinner, breakfast, and transporation back to originating location. This adventure can be extended and combined with the hiking adventure if desired.

Kayaking Adventure:

Our kayaking adventures is an 4-8 hour trip that includes a variety of easy rapids and lake travelling Included is this trip are the following: Kayak and other needed equipment, lunch(optional for 6-8 hour trips), 10-20 photographs, GoPro Cameras are available to rent. If needed, transportion back to starting point is also included. Minimum age for participants is 12 yrs old.

Hiking Adventure:

Our hiking adventures is a short one hour raft ride to a remote hiking area where you will then embark on 1-5 miles hike. You are likely to see lots of wild life so be sure to bring your own camera or rent one of ours. Guides will carry first aid kits, but be sure to bring your own water and snacks. Transportation back to original departure location is provided. Lunch can be purchased at an additional cost.
