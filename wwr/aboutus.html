ABOUT US

WhiteWater Rafting

Let us bring joy to your life by providing a safe environment for adventure. We build team unity and celebrate the individual. We hope you will take the opportunity to come experience our White Water Rafting Adventures.

History

WhiteWater Rafting Company was founded in 1998. We started as a small rafting facilityin the state of Idaho along the Salmon River. also known as the River of No Return. In 2005, we expanded to the Snake River in Southern Idaho.

From 2010-2015, we expanded to other states - the Green River in Wyoming, the Brazos River in Texas, the Colorado River in Colorado, and the Columbian River in Portland, Oregon.
